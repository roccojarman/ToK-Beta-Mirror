# Promotion Gateways

Status: provisional governance architecture.

No candidate becomes canonical because an agent regards it as coherent.

## Readiness assessment

A candidate may be assessed against:

- source preserved
- meaning confirmed
- kind confirmed
- scope assessed
- context assessed
- placement confirmed
- relations assessed
- wording confirmed
- ratified by

An assessment is evidence for a decision. It is not ratification.

## Gateway sequence

1. Candidate is preserved in working state.
2. Extrapolations are separated from source.
3. Kind, scope, context, placement, and relations are proposed.
4. Readiness is assessed.
5. The authorised human reviews the proposal and diff.
6. Ratification identifies the destination and authority.
7. A traceable commit or pull request changes the relevant source repository.
8. WholeTree may be re-impressed by its designated workflow.

Kernel changes require Rocco's ratification. Acorn and Tree authority may later be delegated by governance that has not yet been defined.
